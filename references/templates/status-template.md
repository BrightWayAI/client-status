_Edit to match your firm's preferred structure. The starter ships with four default sections._

---

```
Subject: [Engagement Name] — Status update [date]

Hi [Primary contact first name],

📅 **What we did this week**
- [bullet — lead with verb]
- [bullet]
- [bullet]

💡 **What we learned**
- [bullet — phrased as "what this means for [client]"]

🔭 **What's next**
- [Upcoming meeting or milestone]
- [Decision point or dependency]

🤝 **Anything we need from you**
- [Specific ask, blocker, or decision]
[Or: "All clear on our side this week — appreciate your continued partnership."]

[Optional, if user-context enables thought-leadership shares:]
📚 **A relevant share**
[One-liner: "Saw this and thought of [their initiative]: [link or summary]. [why it matters for them, ≤25 words]"]

[Sign-off per ~/Documents/Claude/voice.md]
[Your name]
```

---

## Customizing this template

Common edits:
- **Add a "Hours invested" line** — if time-tracking is included; placement at top of "What we did" or in a separate KPI line
- **Add a milestone progress bar** — `[Phase 1 ▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓░░] 80%` if project-setup phases are tracked
- **Drop the emoji headers** — some firms find emoji unprofessional; replace with bold headers
- **Add a "Risks / Watching" section** — if your engagements often have evolving risks worth surfacing
- **Add a CTA / next-meeting line** — "See you Tuesday at 2pm" for cadence reinforcement

The plugin reads this template and applies per-client overrides from `references/user-context.md`. Edit freely.
